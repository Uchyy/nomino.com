Flow

A typical round.

  1. Nomino gives you a letter and categories.
  2. Everyone fills out answers against the timer.
  3. Players reveal answers and mark each other.
  4. Any questions or disputes get sent to NIE.
  5. Scores update, and you jump into the next round.
